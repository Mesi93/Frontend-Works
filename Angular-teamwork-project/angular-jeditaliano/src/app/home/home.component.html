~ Restaurant ~

Location

Thanks for stopping by. We are the last authentic Italian restaurant in Budapest, serving delicious Italian cuisine cooked by the best chefs. It only takes a few minutes to browse through our website and check out our menu. We hope you'll soon join us for a superb Italian culinary experience.

A Unique Experience

Our Italian menu is inspired by trips to Italy.You’ll find lots of choice including classic Italian food alongside our own signature take on a favourite dish or ingredient. As well as our made-for-sharing antipasto boards, stone baked pizzas, fresh pasta and desserts, we’ve got vegetarian, vegan and non gluten options so there’s something for everyone.

Lunch

In our restaurant, we serve every afternoon a lunch buffet from 12.00 o’clock till 13.30. You can find for example the soup of the day, several meats, several cheeses, several types of bread, warm dishes (the dishes will change by day), several sweet fillings, several salads, coffee and wine.

Dinner

It is possible to have dinner a la carte in our restaurant. We also serve seasonal dishes. In the months May and June we serve delicious fresh asparagus. In the summer months we presents the real mussels from Zeeland and in the autumn we serve delicious venison dishes. Do you have special wishes or are you following a diet? Please let us know and we will keep this in mind.
